FindMyPet is...

CREATED BY: JAYLENE BEATTS

My name is Jaylene Beatts. I am 15 years old. I have three cats and two dogs. I have lost one of my dogs a number of times so I thought it would be nice to have a pet finding tool. If you have lost a pet and putting up posters isn't getting the word out past your neighborhood, FindMyPet can be used as a virtual poster for your lost dog. People can also post lost dogs they find!
