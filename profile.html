What is a profile?
A pet profile is where you will enter information on a pet that you haave found or lost. Fill a form out with accurate information is very important. It tells other people what to look out for if you have lost a pet. People who have found pets will be able to look for them on the lost pets page and also be able to post the newly found pet.
